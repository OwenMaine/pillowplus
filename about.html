About Pillow Plus Manufacturing

Crafting Comfort, Delivering Quality

Pillow Plus Manufacturing, Inc. is a leading manufacturer of high-quality pillows, committed to providing superior comfort and innovative solutions. Located in North Miami Beach, Florida, we serve a diverse clientele with our extensive range of customizable pillow options.

With decades of experience, we've honed our expertise in crafting pillows that meet the highest standards of quality and durability. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility allows us to produce pillows in large volumes while maintaining meticulous attention to detail.

Our Vision

To be the leading pillow manufacturer, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To craft exceptional pillows that enhance comfort and well-being, while building lasting partnerships with our clients.

We are dedicated to providing exceptional value through:

Premium Materials: Sourcing the finest materials for lasting comfort.

Custom Solutions: Tailoring our services to meet your unique needs.

Expert Craftsmanship: Combining skill and technology for superior quality.

Customer Satisfaction: Prioritizing your needs and exceeding expectations.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Pillow Plus Manufacturing, we are committed to environmentally responsible practices. We continuously seek opportunities to minimize our environmental impact through sustainable sourcing, waste reduction, and energy-efficient operations.
